Accelerated migration, reduced complexity


At the core of DataCORE’s advantage is its AI-driven data migration capability, which operates at three times the speed and half the cost of traditional methods.

This isn’t just faster—it’s transformational. Enterprises can extract, transform, and load ERP data without the usual hurdles of manual intervention, prolonged timelines, or skyrocketing expenses.



Enabling seamless and scalable transformation


By automating and optimizing the data migration journey, DataCORE accelerates enterprise transformation at scale. This unlocks new levels of agility, empowering organizations to rapidly adopt new technologies, implement cloud-native solutions, and stay ahead in a competitive market.

Beyond just migration, DataCORE sets the stage for long-term innovation—ensuring enterprises have clean, accessible, and actionable data to fuel analytics, AI models, and future-ready digital strategies.

Managing vast, fragmented data across multiple systems is a persistent challenge for enterprises.

Disconnected data silos, varied formats, and slow integration processes hinder efficient decision-making and transformation.

DataCORE addresses these challenges and empowers businesses to unlock the true potential of their data.

Frequently asked questions

What is DataCORE and what problems does it solve?

DataCORE is the enterprise data foundation of the CoreOps.AI platform - a harmony engine that unifies fragmented enterprise data into a living, contextual model of your business.
It solves common enterprise data challenges by: Integrating disconnected systems: Seamlessly consuming data from ERP, CRM, BI, and legacy platforms while leaving systems of record untouched. Eliminating fragmentation & schema mismatches: Harmonizing inconsistent formats into a single semantic knowledge graph. Ensuring freshness & trustworthiness: Moving beyond stale batch loads to deliver real-time, contextual data pipelines. Accelerating AI & analytics modernization: Powering advanced use cases with data that’s clean, connected, and ready for AI consumption. With DataCORE, enterprises can trust their AI and analytics platforms to deliver current, accurate, and actionable intelligence.

How does DataCORE streamline data integration?

DataCORE eliminates the complexity of traditional ETL and point-to-point integration by creating a real-time, intelligent data fabric: Intelligent connectors with change-data-capture: Ingests data directly from ERP, CRM, and BI systems in real time—keeping it fresh and reducing migration overhead. Semantic knowledge graph: Maps incoming data into a living graph of business entities and relationships, preserving operational context instead of flattening into rigid schemas. AI-powered transformation: Uses machine learning for automated mapping, classification, and normalization, cutting integration timelines by up to 60% and significantly lowering costs. With DataCORE, enterprises gain seamless, context-rich integration that accelerates modernization while ensuring data quality and trust.

Can DataCORE connect with cloud and on-prem systems?

Yes. DataCORE is cloud-native and hybrid-capable, built for enterprise-scale throughout. It supports distributed processing across cloud and on-prem environments, ensuring secure integration with systems behind corporate firewalls. With horizontal scaling to petabyte-level data volumes, DataCORE maintains high performance and reliability regardless of deployment model.

What are the security protocols within DataCORE?

DataCORE enforces enterprise-grade security and compliance at every layer. All ingested data is tracked through an immutable graph-based lineage mechanism, ensuring full auditability and traceability. Compliance monitoring is built in, with causal reasoning and lineage transparency to meet regulatory requirements. These protocols ensure that DataCORE delivers trusted, verifiable, and compliant data operations across cloud and on-prem environments.

Is DataCORE scalable for enterprise use?

Absolutely. DataCORE is built for enterprise-scale performance. Its cloud-native, distributed architecture enables horizontal scaling across massive data volumes—up to petabytes—without compromising speed or reliability. Whether deployed on cloud, on-premise, or hybrid environments, DataCORE ensures consistent throughput, resilience, and high availability—making it ideal for complex, large-scale enterprise landscapes.
