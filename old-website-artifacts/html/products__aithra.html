Our products

CoreOps.AI delivers a robust product suite designed for seamless integration and rapid deployment. Our AI-powered tools drive efficiency, agility and innovation across the enterprise value chain.

AgentCORE

AgentCORE is designed to transform how enterprises automate their workflows by building, training, and deploying intelligent AI agents.

Discover

These agents work autonomously across ERP, CRM, and other enterprise systems, seamlessly integrating into existing infrastructures. With its intuitive interface and pre-built templates, AgentCORE significantly reduces the complexity of setting up AI-driven automation.

Know more

CORESight

A CXO-level conversational analytics co-pilot seamlessly integrated with your existing enterprise systems for real-time, data-driven decision-making.

Discover

Designed to empower leadership with actionable insights, CORESight breaks data silos and presents unified metrics across the organization. Its intuitive interface allows users to explore, visualize, and interpret data without needing technical expertise.

Know more

DataCORE

An AI-powered ERP data extraction and migration accelerator that seamlessly converts data into secure, accurate & AI-ready formats.

Discover

Built to simplify complex data landscapes, DataCORE ensures consistency while significantly reducing migration time and costs. Its cloud-native architecture supports large-scale data processing with robust security.

Know more

CORETrack

A Fiori-based, cloud-enabled solution that digitizes and automates the entire gate access process across enterprise facilities.

Discover

Designed to handle both inbound and outbound flows, it streamlines the entry and exit of materials, vehicles, and personnel; improving security, traceability, and compliance. 

Know more

COREServ

A cloud-based, end-to-end service management platform designed to optimize the way businesses plan, dispatch, and resolve customer service requests.

Discover

It simplifies how you plan, assign, and complete requests through a powerful service request management system, connecting seamlessly with your existing systems like SAP S/4HANA, SAP CRM, and SAP C4C.

Know more

COREHire

An advanced AI-driven recruitment platform designed to streamline and automate critical hiring workflows.

Discover

Using AI, it analyses resumes, compares them with job descriptions, and gives each candidate a matching score. Instead of screening hundreds of profiles manually, recruiters instantly see who fits the role and who doesn’t.

Know more

Aithra

Aithra brings clarity by understanding questions, interpreting business context, and delivering accurate answers instantly.

Discover

Aithra is an enterprise-grade AI Assistant designed to automate employee and customer interactions. It integrates with your business systems to provide instant, accurate responses.

Know more

Frequently asked questions

What does CoreOps.AI specialize in?

We specialize in delivering enterprise‑grade AI solutions designed to modernize and automate core business operations. This includes ERP implementation and modernization, AI workflow SaaS products (like intelligent supply‑chain, CXO Copilot, Customer 360), and ongoing application and workflow management services. It has a very strong focus on agentic AI for changing workflows across ERP, CRM, and BI systems.

How do CoreOps.AI solutions integrate AI with enterprise systems?

AgentCORE makes it possible for businesses to develop, train, and deploy autonomous AI agents to run independently across current ERP, CRM, and enterprise applications. AgentCORE offers pre‑configured templates, monitoring, and learning, as well as support for integration with SAP's Generative AI Hub.

CORESight is a generative‑AI‑powered analytics co‑pilot: you ask business questions in plain English and it auto‑generates optimized data queries and visualizations. It connects seamlessly with SAP, BI tools, and your enterprise data infrastructure.

DataCORE accelerates ERP data extraction and migration by converting data into AI‑ready formats, managing large volumes seamlessly with secure, scalable cloud architecture, and reducing migration time.

These products connect deeply into legacy and modern enterprise systems without requiring major rip‑and‑replace, embedding intelligence on top of existing infrastructure.

Which industries benefit from CoreOps.AI’s products?

We provide industry-agnostic solutions and manufacturing, retail, electronics, healthcare, professional services and more, have realized considerable advantage. Examples of use cases are supply-chain optimization, GST reconciliation, CRM modernization, strategic analytics, and smart diagnostics.

How can I request a demo of your solutions?

You can ask for a demo through our website from the homepage and the product pages (AgentCORE, CORESight, CoreOps.AI edge, etc.), each provides a "Request a demo" button/or send us an email at marketing@coreops.ai.

What differentiates CoreOps.AI from other AI service providers?

We differentiate on: Agentic AI strategy: We construct independent AI agents (through AgentCORE) that act within enterprise business processes—not automation, but context‑sensitive intelligence that learns constantly. Deep enterprise‑systems integration: Our software is designed to integrate with ERP and CRM systems such as SAP, BI tools, and installed systems through seamless connectors and templates. Speed & cost efficiency: Client results show up to 2x faster development cycles and 25 % cost reductions on modernization efforts. Enterprise‑grade engineering: Founded by veterans of global Fortune 100 tech leadership, and they follow full CI/CD, audit‑trail, versioning and compliance workflows. The AgentCORE CLI is also open‑sourced under a BSL license, inviting community collaboration.
