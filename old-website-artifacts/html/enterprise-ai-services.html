AI strategy consulting

Enterprise-wide AI integration is a journey that requires a vision and a profound understanding of the enterprise needs. The complete lifecycle of AI adoption, from initial assessment to full-scale implementation, needs to be strategized and documented, and altered as per the evolving business vision. Our AI Strategy Consulting services guide enterprises through every stage of their AI journey with focused clarity and a wealth of experience. Here’s how it works:


  • Assessment of current systems: We first evaluate your existing infrastructure and data capabilities to identify AI readiness.
  • Opportunity identification: Our team then pinpoints high-impact AI use cases that are aligned with your business goals.
  • AI adoption roadmap: A tailored, phased plan for seamless AI integration is developed by our experts.
  • Ethical AI & compliance: We ensure all AI solutions meet regulatory standards and ethical guidelines of the industry and the region.

Custom AI development

Every business has its own unique set of challenges and a one-size-fits-all AI cannot be the approach when it comes to enterprise-wide AI integration. We solve specific business challenges with our custom AI solutions by leveraging the power of our core products, AgentCORE and CORESight.


  • AI model & application development: Our team builds bespoke machine learning, NLP, and computer vision models that are aligned with your business needs.
  • AI agent creation: We use AgentCORE to develop and deploy AI agents designed to automate workflow and decision support for your specific business landscape.
  • Conversational AI interfaces: We customize natural language interfaces by leveraging CORESight to answer critical data queries and deliver impactful insights.
  • Enterprise application integration: Our custom AI development service enables embedment of AI capabilities into existing systems for exceptional functionality.

Data services

Data is the foundation of any enterprise’s digital transformation. Our data services, powered by DataCORE, treat your data securely and swiftly to become AI-ready.


  • Data integration and transformation: Our DataCORE product is designed to unify and transform data from disparate sources.
  • Data cleaning and labeling: Our team prepares high-quality datasets for model training.
  • Data pipelines: We build scalable pipelines for model training and real-time inference.
  • Data governance and security: Protecting sensitive data is our priority as we implement robust governance frameworks during the AI integration process.

AI integration

The successful modernization of an enterprise is possible with AI integration across the complete ecosystem, seamlessly and accurately.


  • Production deployment: We deploy AI models and agents into live environments with minimal disruption to your business operations.
  • System integration: Our team connects AI solutions with ERP, CRM, and other enterprise systems to ensure seamless enterprise-wide AI adoption.
  • API and microservices development: We enable scalable AI functionality through custom APIs.
  • User interface design: Our expertise includes creating intuitive interfaces for AI-powered applications, with a focus on ease of use.

AI maintenance & support

A dedicated AI ongoing support team ensures your AI systems remain effective and aligned with evolving business needs.


  • Model monitoring and drift detection: Our services team continuously tracks model performance and data drift.
  • Model retraining and updates: We conduct regular updates to keep AI models accurate and relevant.
  • 24/7 support: We provide round-the-clock assistance for critical AI systems.
  • Issue resolution: Our services team will be available for rapid troubleshooting to maintain system reliability.

Training & education

Frequently asked questions

What types of AI services does CoreOps.AI offer?

We deliver a comprehensive enterprise AI services portfolio spanning three dimensions:
• AI as a Service (AIaaS): ◦ Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) for contextual intelligence ◦ Self-managed AI agents for workflows and operations ◦ Enterprise AI search for knowledge discovery ◦ Text-to-SQL conversational analytics for natural data access • AI Consulting Services: A proven 7-step roadmap—from AI evaluation and data strategy to model development, deployment, and continuous maintenance—ensuring alignment with your business outcomes. • AI Product Suite: ◦ AgentCORE: Enterprise AI agent orchestration ◦ CORESight: Conversational business intelligence & analytics ◦ DataCORE: Harmonized, AI-ready enterprise data layer This end-to-end approach enables enterprises to adopt, scale, and operationalize AI confidently.

How does AI drive business transformation?

AI accelerates transformation across three key dimensions: Automation & Efficiency: Enterprise AI agents automate workflows, reducing manual effort and cutting operational costs and cycle times by up to 25–50%. Data Intelligence & Decision-Making: Conversational analytics (e.g., CORESight) turn natural language queries into actionable insights, uncovering hidden efficiencies and enabling faster, data-driven decisions. Governed & Scalable Innovation: AIaaS solutions come with governance, compliance, and observability frameworks—ensuring enterprise-grade deployments that are trustworthy, transparent, and scalable.

Do you provide AI integration for legacy systems?

Yes. CoreOps.AI enables seamless AI integration even with legacy enterprise infrastructure: AgentCORE for Automation: AI agents interact with ERP, CRM, and other enterprise systems—including older platforms—via pre-built connectors, APIs, and workflow orchestration. Legacy Modernization Consulting: Our AI consulting services design modernization roadmaps, build secure data pipelines, and deliver migration blueprints to layer AI on top of existing configurations—without disrupting business operations.

What’s your approach to AI model training and deployment?

We follow a governed, enterprise-grade approach to AI model development and operations: Model Building & Training: Development uses Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Scikit-Learn within Jupyter notebooks, with controlled experiment tracking (DVC) and versioning to ensure collaboration and reproducibility. Deployment & CI/CD: We deliver production-ready AI through containerized pipelines (Docker) and automated workflows (GitHub Actions, Azure DevOps), with Nginx API gateways and full observability via Prometheus and Grafana. Governance & Compliance: Every model is deployed with role-based access control, audit trails, and reproducible experiments—ensuring trustworthy, compliant, and transparent AI operations at scale.

How can AI help reduce operational costs?

CoreOps.AI reduces enterprise costs by embedding AI across operations: Efficient Automation: AgentCORE automates repetitive workflows, cutting operational cycle times by up to 50% and reducing labor expenses by 25%. Intelligent Data Processing: DataCORE accelerates data migration and normalization—2x faster and at half the cost of traditional methods. Effective Model Management: Enterprise-grade AI practices (experiment versioning, retraining automation, observability) minimize infrastructure waste and optimize team productivity.
