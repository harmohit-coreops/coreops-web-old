COREHire.AI, developed by CoreOps.AI, is an advanced AI-driven recruitment platform designed to streamline and automate critical hiring workflows. The platform focuses on two major functions—resume compatibility scoring and interview scheduling—to improve hiring speed, accuracy, and experience. By automatically analyzing resumes against job descriptions and managing interview logistics, COREHire.AI empowers recruitment teams to make faster, data-informed decisions. This reduces manual effort, improves candidate engagement, and enhances the end-to-end recruitment cycle—all while integrating smoothly with existing enterprise systems.

Key benefits

  • Faster screening
    AI-driven resume analysis delivers instant compatibility scores to accelerate shortlisting.
  • Improved hiring accuracy
    Intelligent skill, experience, and education matching ensures better candidate-job fit.
  • Automated scheduling
    Interview slots are booked automatically based on mutual availability, eliminating back-and-forth coordination.
  • Enhanced candidate experience
    Pre-screening forms and smart engagement ensure seamless communication with candidates.
  • End-to-end workflow integration
    Automates the journey from resume upload to interview scheduling, with future phases covering onboarding and BGV.

Offerings

  • Resume compatibility scoring
  • Automated resume parsing
  • Smart job description matching
  • Pre-screening form automation
  • Outlook integration for candidate communication
  • Intelligent interview slot booking
  • Candidate database with auto-relevance alerts
  • Future enhancements: BGV & offer management

Process flow: Phase 1 – resume screening & interview scheduling

1. Resume upload
The TAG (Talent Acquisition Group) uploads resumes directly into the COREHire.AI portal.


2. Job description input
The recruiter or TAG enters the job description against which candidates are being evaluated.


3. Resume analysis
The AI engine evaluates resumes based on:

  • Skill match
  • Relevant experience
  • Educational background
  • Certifications (if applicable)

4. Output generation
The platform generates:

  • A detailed resume summary
  • An automated compatibility score

5. Score evaluation
If the score is below the required threshold:

  • The resume is saved in the candidate database.
  • TAG is notified when a relevant job opening matches the profile.

6. Pre-screening form
If the resume is a match, a pre-screening form is automatically sent to the candidate via email (Outlook integration).


7. Candidate compatibility check
Once the form is submitted:

  • The system assesses availability and interest.
  • If the candidate is suitable, the process moves to scheduling.

8. Interview scheduling
The system automatically books interview slots based on the availability of both the candidate and the interview panel via integrated calendar access.

Future phase enhancements

Once interviews are cleared, COREHire.AI will also support:


  • Document collection
  • Background verification (BGV) initiation
  • Offer generation and communication

Process flow

Step 1: Service request generation
Customers initiate a request through web, phone, email, or portal.
A ticket is created in SAP (S/4HANA, CRM, or C4C) and replicated to COREServ.
Captured data includes customer details, issue type, product, priority, and SLA.

Step 2: Planning & dispatching
Dispatchers assign tasks using AI-enabled tools like the planning board, skills matrix, and map view.
Tasks are dispatched based on availability, location, and skill level.
Technicians receive jobs on their mobile app with all required details.

Step 3: Field execution & service confirmation
Technicians perform the service, capture job details, add photos, collect customer signatures, and submit final reports — all from their mobile device.
Data flows back to ERP for billing, inventory, and analytics.

Step 4: Feedback & closure
Optional customer satisfaction surveys are triggered upon task completion.
Managers access survey insights, and the ticket is closed in both CSRHS and the backend SAP system.


CORETrack, developed by CoreOps.AI, is a Fiori-based, cloud-enabled solution that digitizes and automates the entire gate access process across enterprise facilities. Designed to handle both inbound and outbound flows, it streamlines the entry and exit of materials, vehicles, and personnel—improving security, traceability, and compliance. With seamless SAP integration, real-time tracking, and minimal manual intervention, the solution ensures that gate operations are not only efficient but also audit-ready. It eliminates paperwork, reduces delays, and keeps all stakeholders informed through smart notifications and digital workflows.

Key benefits

  • Real-time visibility
    Track material and personnel movement live across multiple locations.
  • Enhanced security
    Gate validations, system checks, and document verification reduce unauthorized access.
  • Audit-ready compliance
    Digital recordkeeping supports audits, improves accountability, and eliminates manual registers.
  • Faster gate operations
    Automated gate pass creation and exit approvals reduce wait times and manual intervention.
  • Seamless SAP integration
    Works out-of-the-box with SAP MM, SD, and WM modules, ensuring end-to-end system connectivity.
  • User-friendly fiori interface
    Clean, intuitive screens simplify use for gate operators and security staff alike.

Gate entry and exit – core process flow

1. Gate entry

  • Materials, goods, or personnel arrive at the premises.
  • Security logs details like vehicle number, purpose, and supporting documents (PO, DC).
  • A system-generated gate entry number ensures traceability.

2. Verification

  • Gate entry data is cross-verified with SAP backend (e.g., PO, GR schedule).
  • If matched, the system approves the entry and notifies relevant stakeholders.

3. Gate exit

  • For outbound movements, a gate pass is digitally generated.
  • Items/personnel are verified against the approved exit details and documentation.
  • Exit is confirmed and digitally recorded in the system.

4. Record maintenance

  • All gate entry and exit records are securely stored and accessible for audits.
  • History can be searched, filtered, and reviewed by security or compliance teams.

Frequently asked questions

What enterprise applications do you specialize in?

We specialize in SAP-focused and AI-driven enterprise applications that accelerate digital transformation and operational intelligence, including: ERP deployment & transformation – SAP S/4HANA migrations, SAP BTP adoption, and enterprise data management. AI workflow SaaS solutions – intelligent supply chain, customer 360, and CXO copilot for smarter, automated decision-making. Enterprise annuity services – Application Management Services (AMS), workflow optimization, and professional consulting for sustained value realization. By combining deep SAP expertise with AI innovation, we help enterprises modernize core operations while unlocking new growth opportunities.

Do you offer end-to-end SAP implementation?

Yes. CoreOps.AI provides full-cycle SAP implementation services, covering the entire journey from planning to steady-state operations: Implementation, migration & consulting – S/4HANA, ECC to S/4 transitions, and landscape modernization. Customization & SAP BTP development – Tailored builds, extensions, and seamless integrations. Enablement & training – empowering business and IT teams for faster adoption. Post-go-live AMS & support – continuous monitoring, enhancements, and SLA-driven managed services. This end-to-end approach ensures enterprises not only deploy SAP successfully but also achieve long-term operational efficiency and scalability.

How do you ensure business continuity during migration?

We utilize accelerated, AI-driven delivery approaches to keep disruption to a minimum: Our ERP implementation services focus on AI-facilitated methodologies for rapid, trustworthy migrations. Application of SAP BTP extensions, standard APIs, and integrations prevents significant core changes—enabling smoother upgrades and less downtime. The ongoing management of applications and professional consulting services ensure continuity post-migration.

Can you integrate custom modules into SAP solutions?

At CoreOps.AI, we ensure smooth transitions with minimal disruption by leveraging AI-driven, accelerated delivery approaches: AI-facilitated migration methodologies – Proven frameworks for rapid, reliable, and low-risk ERP transformations. SAP BTP extensions & standard APIs – Integrations are built outside the SAP core, avoiding heavy customizations and enabling smoother upgrades with reduced downtime. Continuous application management & consulting – Post-migration support, monitoring, and professional consulting ensure stability, continuity, and business readiness from day one. This approach ensures that while systems evolve, critical business operations continue uninterrupted.

What support do you offer post-deployment?

We provide a comprehensive post-deployment support framework to ensure long-term stability, optimization, and compliance: Application Management Services (AMS): End-to-end & day-to-day support, proactive monitoring, and maintenance for SAP and AI-driven applications. Workflow management: Continuous improvement of business processes and automation pipelines to drive efficiency. Expert consulting & training: specialized support for SAP modules (e.g., MM, FI, SD) and industry-specific operations such as corporate rail and supply chain. Auditability & compliance monitoring: Built-in observability, audit trails, and regulatory compliance safeguards - especially critical for AI-based modules and custom workflows. This ensures your enterprise applications remain secure, optimized, and future-ready well beyond deployment.
