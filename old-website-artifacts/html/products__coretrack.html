Overview

CORETrack, developed by CoreOps.AI, is a Fiori-based, cloud-enabled solution that digitizes and automates the entire gate access process across enterprise facilities. Designed to handle both inbound and outbound flows, it streamlines the entry and exit of materials, vehicles, and personnel—improving security, traceability, and compliance. With seamless SAP integration, real-time tracking, and minimal manual intervention, the solution ensures that gate operations are not only efficient but also audit-ready. It eliminates paperwork, reduces delays, and keeps all stakeholders informed through smart notifications and digital workflows.

SAP gate entry and exit process

Gate entry (Inbound process)

The inbound gate entry process in SAP begins when a vehicle carrying materials arrives at the facility. The gross weight (vehicle + material) is captured through weighbridge integration, and after unloading, the tare weight (empty vehicle) is recorded to calculate the net material weight. A Goods Receipt is posted in SAP to update stock, and a gate entry document is generated for traceability. Additionally, visitor entry is tracked for compliance, and “dark parcel” cases (materials without prior PO or inbound delivery) are flagged for manual handling.


  • Gross weight of vehicle + materials captured via weighbridge integration
  • Tare weight (empty vehicle) recorded after unloading to calculate net material weight
  • Goods Receipt posted in SAP to update inventory
  • Gate entry document generated; visitor and “dark parcel” scenarios tracked

Stock placement in warehouse

After goods are received, they must be placed in the correct warehouse storage bins. The system assigns materials to bins based on advanced warehouse management system rules (like material type or storage capacity). A transfer document guides warehouse staff for physical placement, and once confirmed, SAP updates the inventory in real-time. This ensures accuracy in stock levels, better traceability, and efficient use of warehouse space.


  • Materials assigned to predefined bins based on warehouse rules
  • Ensures traceability and optimized storage utilization

Gate exit (Outbound process)

The outbound SAP gate exit process ensures that goods leaving the facility are properly documented. It starts with an outbound request such as a sales order or transfer. Materials are picked from bins using SAP’s picking strategies. Vehicle gross and tare weights are recorded to confirm the quantity shipped. Once verified, a Goods Issue is posted in SAP to reduce inventory and update financial records. A gate exit pass and delivery note are then generated to provide official shipment documentation.


  • Outbound process starts with sales/transfer order or delivery request
  • Materials picked from bins using SAP picking strategies
  • Gross and tare weight recorded to confirm shipped material quantity
  • Gate exit pass, delivery note, and goods issue posting complete the process

Stock removal from warehouse

Stock removal refers to taking materials out of bins for outbound deliveries, transfers, or internal consumption. The process begins with a transfer order that specifies which materials should be removed. Warehouse staff perform the removal as per the system instructions. Once the process is confirmed, SAP updates the inventory by reducing the available stock. This ensures accurate inventory records and smooth fulfilment of delivery or internal needs.


  • Confirmation updates SAP system inventory records
  • Ensures accuracy in stock tracking and availability

FAQs

What is CORETrack and how does it integrate with SAP gate entry and exit?

CORETrack is an automated warehouse management system that integrates seamlessly with SAP for gate entry and exit processes, enabling real-time data syncing, weighbridge integration, and traceability for inbound/outbound movements.

Where can CORETrack be used in the SAP gate entry process?

CORETrack can be used at the inbound stage for capturing gross/tare weights via weighbridge integration, generating gate entry documents, posting goods receipts, and tracking visitor parcel scenarios.

How does CORETrack improve the SAP gate exit process?

CORETrack streamlines the outbound process by automating material picking from bins, verifying shipped quantities through weight recordings, posting goods issues, and generating gate exit passes and delivery notes for efficiency.

Can CORETrack replace manual gate registers in SAP-based operations?

Yes, CORETrack replaces manual gate registers with automated tracking, reducing errors and unauthorized access while providing full traceability in SAP-integrated operations.

Does CORETrack support real-time data syncing with SAP?

Yes, CORETrack supports real-time data syncing with SAP to update inventory, stock levels, and financial records instantly during gate entry, exit, and warehouse movements.

How does CORETrack help in compliance and security with SAP gate processes?

CORETrack enhances compliance and security by automating verification, flagging unauthorized entries/exits, and maintaining audit trails for all gate processes in SAP, mitigating risks from manual handling.

Is CORETrack useful only for inbound logistics with SAP?

No, CORETrack is useful for both inbound (gate entry, goods receipt) and outbound (gate exit, stock removal) logistics, as well as internal warehouse management in SAP.

How does CORETrack help with weighbridge integration in SAP?

CORETrack integrates weighbridges directly with SAP to capture gross and tare weights automatically, calculate net material quantities, and ensure accurate documentation during gate entry and exit.

What industries can benefit from using CORETrack with SAP gate entry and exit?

Industries such as manufacturing, logistics, retail, pharmaceuticals, and automotive can benefit from CORETrack's SAP integration for efficient gate management, inventory accuracy, and operational compliance.
