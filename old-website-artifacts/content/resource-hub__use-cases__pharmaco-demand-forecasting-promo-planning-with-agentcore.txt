PharmaCo – Demand forecasting & promo planning with AgentCORE

In a highly competitive pharma market, accurate demand planning is the difference between capturing growth and losing ground. For PharmaCo, balancing inventory availability, promo effectiveness, and regional prescribing trends was becoming increasingly complex, yet their forecasting was still being done on Excel.

Challenge

PharmaCo’s sales teams relied on Excel-based forecasts and gut feel. Critical demand drivers—such as promotions, flu season spikes, and regional prescribing patterns—were missing. This led to:

Missed opportunities due to understocking in high-demand zones.
Excess inventory and SKU wastage when demand was overestimated.
Inefficient marketing spends with little visibility into promo impact.

Business impact: Revenue leakage, excess working capital locked in inventory, and poor coordination across sales, supply chain, and marketing.

AgentCORE in action

Solution: AgentCORE generated a 12-week demand forecast for medicine xyz across North & South zones, factoring in seasonal surges and promotion calendars. The trained model was deployed as an API, enabling the CRM team to query forecasts live.

AgentCORE workflow
1. Data ingestion & preparation
Upload sales history, promotion calendars, physician prescribing data, and seasonal indicators.
Auto-engineer features: Promo flags, lagged sales, moving averages, and holiday effects.
2. Model training
Auto-select best-fit algorithm (SARIMAX, Prophet, or ML-based models).
Auto-generate Python code + configuration inside AgentCORE UI.
Train, validate, and benchmark with MAPE & RMSE.
Save trained model with complete metadata (dataset, parameters, version).
3. Model deployment
One click: “Promote Model” in AgentCORE UI.
Model is containerized and deployed automatically.
REST API endpoint available for CRM & ERP integration.
MLOps built-in
Experiment tracking: Every run logged with dataset, parameters, metrics.
Model registry & versioning: Complete traceability.
One-click deployment: No DevOps required.
Monitoring & auto-retraining: Accuracy tracked, retraining scheduled.
Safe rollback: Revert to last stable version instantly.
Customer impact
90% faster deployment: From training to hosted API in one click.
25–30% better accuracy: Capturing promo & seasonal effects.
Real-time access: CRM & ERP teams query forecasts live.
Cross-team alignment: Single source of truth for demand planning.

AgentCORE turned PharmaCo’s intuition-driven forecasting into a data-driven, automated, and live forecasting system—improving accuracy, speed, and business agility.
