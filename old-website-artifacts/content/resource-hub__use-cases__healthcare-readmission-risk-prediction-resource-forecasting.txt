Healthcare – readmission risk prediction & resource forecasting
Challenge

Hospitals lack the foresight to identify high-risk patients early and struggle to align staff with patient demand. Siloed EMR, lab, and ops systems delay action, increase readmissions, and overload clinical teams.

CORESight in action

Query: “From past 2 years’ EMR data, find patients discharged last week with >70% readmission risk. Forecast ER visits next Tuesday. Flag cardiology staffing gaps.”

CORESight workflow
Extractor agent pulls EMR, EHR, and lab data (demographics, diagnoses, labs).
Planner & coder agents run predictive models for readmissions & ER demand.
Business agent delivers:
3 high-risk patients flagged for early intervention.
ER visits next Tuesday: 85 forecasted.
Cardiology short 1 RN during peak hours.
Customer impact
Up to 40% fewer readmissions — Intervene proactively on high-risk discharges.
25% better staff utilization — Forecast demand, adjust schedules in advance.
90% faster clinical insights — From manual reports to real-time NL queries.
Unified clinical view — Combine EMR, lab, and ops data seamlessly.
Fully compliant — Built-in security & health data governance.
CORESight advantage

CORESight delivers real-time, AI-powered clinical intelligence. From readmission risk prediction to staff forecasting, it empowers hospitals to act faster, improve outcomes, and manage resources with precision — all via natural language.
