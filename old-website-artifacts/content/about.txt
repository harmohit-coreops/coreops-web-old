Our story
Driving the future with AI

While leading technology businesses across fortune 100 companies in 100+ countries, the CoreOps.AI founders witnessed enterprise pain points up close and repeatedly. Having navigated multiple waves of tech disruption, they recognized AI as the next big shift in enterprise operations. CoreOps.AI was born from that insight: to transform core enterprise challenges into opportunities for growth.

Where insight meets execution

With a 60+ member team drawn from top global and Indian tech firms, CoreOps.AI combines deep domain knowledge with execution muscle. Powered by our proprietary platform AgentCORE and supported by our products CORESight and DataCORE, our AI-led solutions enrich business processes, empower teams, and accelerate modernization—quietly, effectively, and at scale. We’re here to help enterprises unlock their full potential with AI at the helm.

The dandelion effect

At CoreOps.AI, our logo is the dandelion that symbolizes our vision—resilient, adaptable, and effortlessly scalable. Like its seeds, our AI solutions embed easily across systems, spreading intelligence with minimal friction. Inspired by neural networks in nature, we design tools that can learn and adapt independently —enriching processes, amplifying decisions, and unlocking new possibilities for businesses. This is the dandelion effect: quiet, powerful, and built for impact.

Rooted in purpose, powered by AI.
Vision

CoreOps.AI is building the AI operating system for enterprises - accelerating modernization by 50% and cutting costs by 25% through intelligent automation, data orchestration, and legacy transformation.

Mission

CoreOps.AI leverages enterprise AI solutions to unify data, automate processes/workflows and provide predictive insights enabling organisations to optimise operations, enhance decision making and achieve seamless scalability.

At CoreOps.AI, we believe in the quiet power of transformation—like the dandelion that seeds change wherever it lands.

Inspired by this symbol of resilience and growth, our enterprise AI solutions are designed to take root seamlessly, enrich core operations, and spark innovation across your business.

Founded by industry veterans with deep B2B expertise and a track record of scaling global tech businesses, CoreOps.AI brings the power of agentic AI to modernize legacy systems, accelerate digital transformation, and shape the future of intelligent enterprises.

Our purpose is to apply enterprise AI solutions to integrate data, automate processes, and provide predictive insights—to enable organizations to optimize functions, improve decision-making, and achieve seamless scalability. They seek to transform core systems and infuse intelligence into operations to drive transformative business results.
