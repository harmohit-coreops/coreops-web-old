Fertilizer co. – AI-driven demand forecasting & stock rebalancing
Challenge

Inventory and distribution across multiple plants are slow and reactive due to siloed systems and manual coordination. Demand shifts go unnoticed, causing frequent stockouts, overstock, and logistics inefficiencies.

CORESight in action

Query: “Analyze ‘Urea Gold’ inventory across all plants. Forecast 4-week demand by region. Rebalance stock from Plant A to Plant B. Alert logistics if transfer >500 tons.”

CORESight workflow
Extractor agent pulls inventory, production, logistics data from SAP & others.
Planner & coder agents generate and run code for demand forecast & rebalancing.
Executor agent validates logic.
Business agent responds: “Plant A: 1200T (200T surplus). Plant B: 300T (300T deficit). Transfer 300T advised.”
Customer impact
25% fewer stockouts & overstock — AI ensures optimal inventory across plants.
90% faster planning — Natural language replaces multi-day manual workflows.
30% more accurate forecasts — ML predicts regional demand shifts early.
Autonomous sync — Aligns supply chain ops without manual follow-ups.
Enterprise-ready — Secure, governed, SAP-integrated, cloud/on-prem.
CORESight advantage

Transforms complex inventory decisions into real-time, intelligent actions. With seamless ERP & supply chain integration, CORESight uses natural language to trigger autonomous analysis and planning — securely, and at scale.
