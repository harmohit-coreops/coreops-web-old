One of the fastest growing technology advisor chooses CoreOps.AI for SAP system assessment to enable end-to-end automation
Business challenges
Key processes were not fully integrated into SAP, causing disconnects.
Manual workarounds led to inefficiencies and data inconsistency.
Missing business data points limited analytics and strategic decision-making.
Our solution
Conducted SAP system assessment covering functional, technical, and data layers.
Mapped end-to-end business processes across SAP and surround systems.
Identified gaps in automation and data capture across CRM, Q2O, and O2C.
Recommended a roadmap for full process integration and enterprise-wide analytics enablement.
Key benefits
End-to-end process automation – reduced manual effort and errors.
Real-time data visibility for better decision-making.
Stronger compliance and audit readiness.
Analytics-driven insights to unlock new opportunities.
