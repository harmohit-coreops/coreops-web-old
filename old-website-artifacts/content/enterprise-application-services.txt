COREHire.AI, developed by CoreOps.AI, is an advanced AI-driven recruitment platform designed to streamline and automate critical hiring workflows. The platform focuses on two major functions—resume compatibility scoring and interview scheduling—to improve hiring speed, accuracy, and experience. By automatically analyzing resumes against job descriptions and managing interview logistics, COREHire.AI empowers recruitment teams to make faster, data-informed decisions. This reduces manual effort, improves candidate engagement, and enhances the end-to-end recruitment cycle—all while integrating smoothly with existing enterprise systems.

Faster screening

Frequently asked questions
What enterprise applications do you specialize in?

We specialize in SAP-focused and AI-driven enterprise applications that accelerate digital transformation and operational intelligence, including:
ERP deployment & transformation – SAP S/4HANA migrations, SAP BTP adoption, and enterprise data management.
AI workflow SaaS solutions – intelligent supply chain, customer 360, and CXO copilot for smarter, automated decision-making.
Enterprise annuity services – Application Management Services (AMS), workflow optimization, and professional consulting for sustained value realization.
By combining deep SAP expertise with AI innovation, we help enterprises modernize core operations while unlocking new growth opportunities.

Do you offer end-to-end SAP implementation?

Yes. CoreOps.AI provides full-cycle SAP implementation services, covering the entire journey from planning to steady-state operations:
Implementation, migration & consulting – S/4HANA, ECC to S/4 transitions, and landscape modernization.
Customization & SAP BTP development – Tailored builds, extensions, and seamless integrations.
Enablement & training – empowering business and IT teams for faster adoption.
Post-go-live AMS & support – continuous monitoring, enhancements, and SLA-driven managed services.
This end-to-end approach ensures enterprises not only deploy SAP successfully but also achieve long-term operational efficiency and scalability.

How do you ensure business continuity during migration?

We utilize accelerated, AI-driven delivery approaches to keep disruption to a minimum:
Our ERP implementation services focus on AI-facilitated methodologies for rapid, trustworthy migrations.
Application of SAP BTP extensions, standard APIs, and integrations prevents significant core changes—enabling smoother upgrades and less downtime.
The ongoing management of applications and professional consulting services ensure continuity post-migration.

Can you integrate custom modules into SAP solutions?

At CoreOps.AI, we ensure smooth transitions with minimal disruption by leveraging AI-driven, accelerated delivery approaches:
AI-facilitated migration methodologies – Proven frameworks for rapid, reliable, and low-risk ERP transformations.
SAP BTP extensions & standard APIs – Integrations are built outside the SAP core, avoiding heavy customizations and enabling smoother upgrades with reduced downtime.
Continuous application management & consulting – Post-migration support, monitoring, and professional consulting ensure stability, continuity, and business readiness from day one.
This approach ensures that while systems evolve, critical business operations continue uninterrupted.

What support do you offer post-deployment?

We provide a comprehensive post-deployment support framework to ensure long-term stability, optimization, and compliance:
Application Management Services (AMS): End-to-end & day-to-day support, proactive monitoring, and maintenance for SAP and AI-driven applications.
Workflow management: Continuous improvement of business processes and automation pipelines to drive efficiency.
Expert consulting & training: specialized support for SAP modules (e.g., MM, FI, SD) and industry-specific operations such as corporate rail and supply chain.
Auditability & compliance monitoring: Built-in observability, audit trails, and regulatory compliance safeguards - especially critical for AI-based modules and custom workflows.
This ensures your enterprise applications remain secure, optimized, and future-ready well beyond deployment.
