Privacy policy

At CoreOps.AI, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users and their data. This Privacy Policy outlines the information we collect, how we use and share that information, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Information collection: We collect personal information such as name, email address, and company information when you create an account or sign up for our services. We also collect usage data to improve our platform and user experience.
Use of information: We use the collected information to provide, maintain, and improve our services, communicate with our users, and enforce our policies. We may also use aggregate, non-identifiable data for research and marketing purposes.
Data sharing: We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. We may share information with our service providers or as required by law enforcement or legal processes.
Data security: We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
Cookies and tracking technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to improve our website and services. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
User rights: You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information, as well as the right to opt-out of certain data uses. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@coreops.ai
Children’s privacy: Our services are not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
Updates to this policy: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes. By using our website or services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at privacy@coreops.ai
