CORESight transforms inventory optimisation for a leading agri supply chain
Business challenges
High working capital: Slow moving & obsolete inventory across multiple plants.
Outdated SQL reports limiting access to real-time inventory insights.
Operational bottlenecks – Data access restricted to tech teams, slow decision making for business users.
Our solution
CORESight, deployed on Oracle Cloud (OCI), ensures secure, scalable performance with native Oracle EBS integration—real-time data stays within the enterprise cloud.
Business teams use natural language to access data, removing SQL and tech dependencies.
CORESight delivers AI-driven insights on demand, accelerating decisions across procurement, planning, and operations.
Key benefits
Improved cash flow through early action on non-moving inventory.
Enhanced liquidity by-eliminating inventory inefficiencies.
Boosted supply chain responsiveness through real-time inventory insights.

“With CORESight, we moved from static reports to real-time intelligence. What used to take hours, sometimes days, is now available instantly in plain language. It’s transformed how our teams make inventory decisions and unlocked significant operational efficiency.”
— CIO, Leading Agri-Input enterprise
