Frequently asked questions
What is CORESight used for?

CORESight is a conversational, AI-driven analytics platform that empowers users to ask complex business questions in plain English and receive real-time insights - no coding or delays. It harnesses generative AI to translate natural language into optimized data queries, auto-generates visualizations, and connects seamlessly with systems like SAP and your BI stack.

How does CORESight enhance SAP BI and analytics?

Powered by generative AI, CORESight translates natural-language queries into optimized data queries that extract insights from SAP systems and your broader BI stack - no SQL required.
It auto-generates visualizations, presenting the most appropriate dashboards and charts instantly making analysis quicker and more actionable.
CORESight integrates seamlessly with SAP and your BI tools, unlocking intelligent, conversational access to your existing infrastructure.

Is CORESight compatible with non-SAP systems?

Yes. CORESight is built to integrate with a wide range of data sources beyond SAP; specifically:
It connects to data sources such as SAP, Oracle, CSVs, and S3, enabling data extraction across structured and unstructured systems.
CORESight’s multi-agent architecture - including planner, extractor, transformer, coder, executor, and business agents - handles data ingestion, normalization, secure execution, and insight synthesis across diverse environments.
The platform is LLM-agnostic by design: leveraging a “Model Context Protocol (MCP),” it can integrate with OpenAI, Gemini, Claude, or self-hosted models - offering full flexibility across AI stacks.
For enterprise workflows, CORESight supports integration with tools like Slack and Teams, enabling seamless extension into broader organizational systems.

Can I try a demo or request a pilot?

Yes. You can request a demo or discuss a pilot directly through our website. The homepage, product pages (AgentCORE, CORESight, CoreOps.AI edge), and the contact section provide a “Request a Demo” button. Once submitted, our team will connect with you to schedule a walkthrough or tailor a pilot program to your business needs.

What kind of data insights does CORESight provide?

CORESight delivers real-time, business-critical insights across structured and unstructured data sources. These include:
Descriptive analytics: Profitability and margin breakdowns, sales KPIs, regional trends, and inventory movements.
Predictive & prescriptive insights: Demand forecasting, procurement optimization, vendor performance evaluation, and proactive recommendations (e.g., alternative suppliers or inventory reallocation).
Contract and procurement intelligence: Extraction and comparison of terms, clauses, and supplier metrics from contracts, PDFs, and other documents.
Audit-ready transparency: All insights are backed by DataCORE’s semantic knowledge graph with full lineage, audit trails, and reasoning visibility.
