COREHire.AI, developed by CoreOps.AI, is an advanced AI-driven recruitment platform designed to streamline and automate critical hiring workflows. The platform focuses on two major functions—resume compatibility scoring and interview scheduling—to improve hiring speed, accuracy, and experience.

By automatically analyzing resumes against job descriptions and managing interview logistics, COREHire.AI empowers recruitment teams to make faster, data-informed decisions. This reduces manual effort, improves candidate engagement, and enhances the end-to-end recruitment cycle—all while integrating smoothly with existing enterprise systems.

How does COREHire work?

COREHire accelerates hiring by automating two key steps: resume-job compatibility scoring and interview scheduling. It intelligently matches candidates to roles, reducing manual screening, and streamlines interview logistics for recruiters and candidates alike. By integrating with enterprise HR systems, COREHire.AI improves hiring speed, accuracy, and candidate engagement - enhancing the overall recruitment experience.

How does COREServ work?

COREServ is a cloud-based, end-to-end service management platform that streamlines how businesses plan, dispatch, and resolve customer service requests. With intuitive task workflows, real-time collaboration tools, and seamless ERP integration, COREServ enables service agents, field technicians, and managers to deliver faster, more efficient, and high-quality customer support.

How does CORETrack work?

CORETrack is a fiori-based, cloud-enabled solution that digitizes and automates the gate access process across enterprise facilities. It manages inbound and outbound flows of materials, vehicles, and personnel, enhancing security, traceability, and compliance while reducing manual intervention and operational bottlenecks.
