What types of AI services does CoreOps.AI offer?

We deliver a comprehensive enterprise AI services portfolio spanning three dimensions:

•
AI as a Service (AIaaS):
◦
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) for contextual intelligence
◦
Self-managed AI agents for workflows and operations
◦
Enterprise AI search for knowledge discovery
◦
Text-to-SQL conversational analytics for natural data access
•
AI Consulting Services:
A proven 7-step roadmap—from AI evaluation and data strategy to model development, deployment, and continuous maintenance—ensuring alignment with your business outcomes.
•
AI Product Suite:
◦
AgentCORE: Enterprise AI agent orchestration
◦
CORESight: Conversational business intelligence & analytics
◦
DataCORE: Harmonized, AI-ready enterprise data layer
This end-to-end approach enables enterprises to adopt, scale, and operationalize AI confidently.

How does AI drive business transformation?

AI accelerates transformation across three key dimensions:
Automation & Efficiency: Enterprise AI agents automate workflows, reducing manual effort and cutting operational costs and cycle times by up to 25–50%.
Data Intelligence & Decision-Making: Conversational analytics (e.g., CORESight) turn natural language queries into actionable insights, uncovering hidden efficiencies and enabling faster, data-driven decisions.
Governed & Scalable Innovation: AIaaS solutions come with governance, compliance, and observability frameworks—ensuring enterprise-grade deployments that are trustworthy, transparent, and scalable.

Do you provide AI integration for legacy systems?

Yes. CoreOps.AI enables seamless AI integration even with legacy enterprise infrastructure:
AgentCORE for Automation: AI agents interact with ERP, CRM, and other enterprise systems—including older platforms—via pre-built connectors, APIs, and workflow orchestration.
Legacy Modernization Consulting: Our AI consulting services design modernization roadmaps, build secure data pipelines, and deliver migration blueprints to layer AI on top of existing configurations—without disrupting business operations.

What’s your approach to AI model training and deployment?

We follow a governed, enterprise-grade approach to AI model development and operations:
Model Building & Training: Development uses Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Scikit-Learn within Jupyter notebooks, with controlled experiment tracking (DVC) and versioning to ensure collaboration and reproducibility.
Deployment & CI/CD: We deliver production-ready AI through containerized pipelines (Docker) and automated workflows (GitHub Actions, Azure DevOps), with Nginx API gateways and full observability via Prometheus and Grafana.
Governance & Compliance: Every model is deployed with role-based access control, audit trails, and reproducible experiments—ensuring trustworthy, compliant, and transparent AI operations at scale.

How can AI help reduce operational costs?

CoreOps.AI reduces enterprise costs by embedding AI across operations:
Efficient Automation: AgentCORE automates repetitive workflows, cutting operational cycle times by up to 50% and reducing labor expenses by 25%.
Intelligent Data Processing: DataCORE accelerates data migration and normalization—2x faster and at half the cost of traditional methods.
Effective Model Management: Enterprise-grade AI practices (experiment versioning, retraining automation, observability) minimize infrastructure waste and optimize team productivity.
