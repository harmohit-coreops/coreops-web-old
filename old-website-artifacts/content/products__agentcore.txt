Frequently asked questions
What is AgentCORE and how does it work?

AgentCORE is an integrated, all-in-one platform for AgentOps that empowers enterprises to build, train, and deploy intelligent AI agents that operate autonomously across ERP, CRM, and enterprise systems. It features an intuitive interface with pre-built templates for multi-agent collaboration, enabling smarter execution across complex workflows. The platform integrates with SAP’s Generative AI hub and continuously learns and improves—turning routine operations into intelligent action. AgentCORE also accelerates time to market by reducing model development and deployment time by half, delivers unified visibility across stakeholders with one dashboard, supports the full ML lifecycle in a seamless platform, and provides automated CI/CD pipelines for faster, smarter deployments.

How can AgentCORE improve customer service operations?

Autonomous AI agents streamlining workflows: AgentCORE empowers smart agents that work across CRM and ERP environments, automatically performing mundane tasks such as ticket triaging, escalations, and follow-ups—enhancing response time and minimizing manual labor.

Context-aware decision-making: Agents understand real‑time business context, detect patterns, forecast issues, and personalise interactions—transforming static service systems into proactive support systems.

Unified tools, faster deployment: With a no‑code interface, pre‑built templates, and CI/CD pipelines, service teams can bundle training, deployment, and monitoring without heavy infrastructure lift—cutting development cycles in half and reducing operational costs by 25%.

Is AgentCORE compatible with existing CRM platforms?

Yes, AgentCORE is designed to integrate across CRM systems smoothly:
Designed for CRM integration: AgentCORE is specifically designed to integrate with CRM platforms so that agents can act autonomously across CRM and enterprise systems.
End-to-end integration: The platform supports seamless integration across the full ML lifecycle in a unified environment, working with enterprise systems such as CRM.

Can AgentCORE integrate with SAP and other enterprise platforms?

Yes, AgentCORE is designed for enterprise system integration:
It integrates natively with SAP’s Generative AI hub, enabling continuous learning and improvement within SAP environments.
It supports real-time integration with SAP systems (such as ERP, S/4HANA, CRM, and C4C) through its DataCORE layer for unified data exchange and smart agent execution.
AgentCORE can be securely deployed on-premise behind corporate firewalls while also connecting with orchestrators in the cloud—ideal for organizations maintaining on-site SAP infrastructure.

How do I get started with AgentCORE?

You can request a demo or fill out the contact form on the AgentCORE product page or the Contact Us section.
