CORESight uncovers hidden revenue leaks for a leading electronics brand
Business challenges
Affordability programs (EMIs) were scaling fast but lacked real-time oversight.
Manual, sample-based audits couldn’t detect hidden misuse across thousands of retail points.
Gaps in monitoring – impacted margins & risking compliance.
Our solution
CORESight brought together data across sales, claims & retail ops into a single, intelligent view.
Surfaced early warning signs – unusual usage patterns, claim anomalies, partner-level red flags.
Delivered real-time alerts and decision-ready insights to frontline and compliance teams.
Enabled the brand to govern affordability schemes at scale—proactively, not reactively.
Key benefits
Identified 3x more fraud signals than traditional audit.
Reduced investigation turnaround time by over 60%.
Prevented revenue loss across key Tier-2/3 retail zones.
Strengthened compliance without adding manual overhead.

“We needed more than audits and static rules—something intelligent, scalable, and real-time. CORESight brings exactly that. It’s helping us uncover hidden inefficiencies, protect margins, and act faster with confidence.” — Senior Director, Retail Operations, Global Electronics Brand
