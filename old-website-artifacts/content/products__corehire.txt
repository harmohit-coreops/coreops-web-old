COREHire: Smarter hiring through AI-driven resume analysis & end-to-end candidate tracking

Hiring doesn’t start with the interview — it starts the moment a candidate uploads their resume. COREHire is designed to make this very first step smarter, faster, and more accurate. Using AI, it analyses resumes, compares them with job descriptions, and gives each candidate a matching score. Instead of screening hundreds of profiles manually, recruiters instantly see who fits the role and who doesn’t.

Once a candidate is shortlisted, COREHire takes care of the next steps too — from scheduling interviews to tracking every stage of the hiring process right up to onboarding. It brings structure, transparency, and accountability to the entire recruitment journey.

Frequently asked questions
How does COREHire use AI to analyze resumes?

COREHire’s AI engine automatically scans each resume as soon as it is uploaded. It extracts key skills, experience, and qualifications, compares them with the job description, and generates a clear matching score.

Can COREHire manage interview scheduling automatically?

Yes. COREHire streamlines interview management by suggesting suitable time slots, sending automated reminders to candidates and interviewers, and storing all interview notes, feedback, and outcomes in one place. This eliminates manual follow‑ups and ensures smooth coordination.

Does COREHire track the entire candidate journey end‑to‑end?

Absolutely. COREHire maintains a detailed progress timeline for every candidate — from application received, resume screening, and interview scheduling, to feedback collection, offer rollout, and onboarding. Every step is recorded so no candidate ever slips through the cracks.

Why do companies prefer using COREHire for recruitment?

Companies choose COREHire because it brings clarity, consistency, and speed to the hiring process. AI‑based scoring reduces bias and human errors, automated workflows shorten hiring timelines, and centralized tracking improves team coordination. The result: better matches, smarter hiring, and a seamless candidate experience from start to finish.
