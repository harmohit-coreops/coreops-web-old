Retail – Customer churn prediction and retention planning with AgentCORE

In retail, retaining premium customers is far more cost-effective than acquiring new ones. Yet many retailers still rely on blanket loyalty campaigns, sending discounts and offers to all premium customers, without knowing who is truly at risk of leaving.

Challenge

RetailCo faced broad, inefficient retention campaigns. Without predictive insights, they struggled to identify customers likely to churn. This resulted in:

High marketing spend with low ROI.
Poor personalization, leading to disengagement.
Missed warning signals like reduced visits, declining spend, or repeated complaints.

Business impact: Unnecessary costs, lower retention, and reduced lifetime value (LTV).

AgentCORE in action

Solution: AgentCORE predicted 60-day churn risk for premium customers and deployed the model as a live API. Call center agents and marketers could now fetch churn scores instantly, enabling targeted, personalized retention strategies.

AgentCORE workflow
1. Data ingestion & preparation
Data sourced from CRM, POS, loyalty app, and complaint systems.
Uploaded via CSV into AgentCORE.
Features engineered:
Transactions: Frequency, recency, average spend.
Engagement: App logins, campaign responses, loyalty redemptions.
Support: Number of complaints, resolution times.
2. Model training
Gradient boosting classifier trained to detect complex churn patterns.
Feature engineering included RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) scores.
Model validated with metrics such as AUC and precision-recall.
Trained model stored as .pkl with dataset + hyperparameter metadata.
3. Deployment & API serving
Model registered in AgentCORE model registry.
One-click deployment as REST API, instantly consumable across CRM and call center systems.
MLOps built-in
Experiment tracking: Datasets, parameters, metrics logged for auditability.
Model registry & versioning: Every churn model version tagged and tracked.
One-click deployment: .pkl → REST API in minutes.
Monitoring & drift detection: Auto-detects performance drop, retraining scheduled.
Safe rollback: Instantly revert to a stable model if needed.
Customer impact
30% churn reduction: Early, targeted interventions.
25% lower marketing spend: Focused only on at-risk customers.
Higher LTV: Personalized engagement for premium customers.
Instant insights: Churn scores available in <1s across CRM, call center, and marketing platforms.

In short: AgentCORE transformed RetailCo’s mass-market loyalty program into a precision-targeted retention engine—cutting costs while boosting customer lifetime value.
