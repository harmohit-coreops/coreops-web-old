From fragmented to future-ready: A diversified enterprise engaged in real estate development and education’s cloud-driven finance overhaul by CoreOps.AI
Business challenges

Finance operations spread across disparate platforms leading to:

Delayed reconciliations.
Unwanted or duplicate payments.
Poor visibility across vendors and banks.

This fragmentation led financial losses, impacting profitability and control.

Our solution
Unified SAP S/4HANA Public Cloud to streamline all finance and payment operations.
Integrated bank systems and vendor workflows to eliminate payment fragmentation.
Automated reconciliation and approvals to prevent duplicate or unintended payments.
Deployed rapidly with tailored training and hypercare, ensuring smooth adoption across teams.
Key benefits
Unified financial view better control and compliance.
Faster decision-making– real-time reporting and analytics.
Improved scalability with cloud-first infrastructure.
Reduced manual effort with automated, streamlined processes.

“Partnering with CoreOps.AI for our SAP S/4HANA Public Cloud journey has truly transformative. Their team brought clarity, speed, and deep expertise; helping us unify operations and gain real-time financial visibility across the group.” — Managing Director, of a diversified enterprise engaged in real estate development and education.
